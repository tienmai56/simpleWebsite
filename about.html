Hello,

My name is Tien and I'm a Global Sholar at Babson college, currently holding senior status and majoring in Information technology management and Business analytics.

I got into product management by accident. After I worked in Fidelity Investment (US) as a technology intern, I realized that I'm obsessed with using a user-centric development process to create products that improve people's lives.

Building things has always been what keeps me up at night. In my sophoreyear I founded SheCodes Vietnam, which was awarded by UN Women and CSDS (2020) as a pioneer organization to promote gender equality in the country. Our mission is to support Vietnamese female professionals to learn more about technology through education and various career development programs.

With over 9000+ followers, our team comprising of many young ambitious leaders is building many programs and products that fuel our mission. You can learn more about all the cool things we are doing here!

Coming from a small town in Vietnam, I was very fortunate to be awared full-tuition scholarship to pursue my undergrad studies in the US. During my years in college, I have worked and lived in the US, Vietnam, China, and Singapore.

Outside work, I really enjoy teaching myself how to code or learning about new technologies through online course. I also love writing. I run my blog on medium where I wrote for a number of publications on the topic of product managenent. I also co-started a blog with my friend, where we research on market trends and interesting products, then publish our findings to our subscribers.

To keep my head clear and free from stress, I train different combative sports such as Muay Thai and Brazilian jit-jitsu. I would sign up for local competitions when possible :)
